Our Products and Services

Health Records and Research

Powerful tool which supports medical researching. Create your own research model, integrate it with many different systems, collaborate with other professionals and teams. See the results graphically in many ways you want.

Orthoses and Prostheses Management

Manage your use of Orthoses and Prostheses, from surgery plan to post-surgery assistance to patients. Visually choose your O&P list, receive updates from suppliers, check availability and authorization from hospitals and healthcare plans. See statistics of using graphically. Integrate and collaborate with "Health Records and Research" tool.

ERP Integration consulting

You can use our applications independently of any external system. On the other hand, it's also possible to integrate them to many different systems. We have many and many years of experience on systems integration and we do it the best way possible. We use the latest technologies and management practices.

Medical Research consulting
